### 9. FITNESS

**Today's insight:** A systematic review and meta-analysis (PMC9653533) found that 8–12 weeks of combined heavy resistance training and plyometrics performed 2–3 times per week significantly improves running economy in trained runners. The mechanism is neuromuscular — fibers activate more synchronously, reducing the energy cost per stride without increasing VO2max. If you run regularly, two lifting sessions per week isn't accessory work: it's primary performance training.

### 10. MEP ENGINEERING

**Today's technical item:** California's 2026 Energy Code (effective January 1, 2026) now mandates higher efficiency thresholds for all new residential and commercial construction, with a hard push toward heat pump adoption for space and water heating. Critically, higher-GWP refrigerants — including R-410A in many new equipment categories — are phased out from 2026. MEP engineers specifying HVAC on California-permitted projects must now select A2L refrigerant equipment (e.g., R-32, R-454B) or risk compliance failures at commissioning. This is a direct specification-level change that affects everything from equipment selection to equipment room ventilation design under ASHRAE 15.
