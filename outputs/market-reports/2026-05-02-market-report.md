### 10. MEP ENGINEERING

**Today's technical item:** ASHRAE's May 2026 journal update focuses on revisions to Standard 62.1 (Ventilation and Acceptable Indoor Air Quality), specifically new guidance for enclosed parking garages — a perennial design challenge for MEP engineers.
