### 9. FITNESS

**Today's insight:** Research consistently shows that adding strength training 2–3 times per week for 8–12 weeks improves running economy by approximately 3–4% in trained endurance runners, without meaningful changes in body composition. The mechanism is neuromuscular efficiency — stronger legs require less energy at a given pace. For maximum stimulus, slow your lowering phase to 3–4 seconds and pause at the stretched position; this eccentric emphasis drives more muscle growth signalling and reduces injury risk versus fast, sloppy reps. *(Sources: Springer Nature Sports Medicine meta-analysis; MDPI Applied Sciences 2025)*

### 10. MEP ENGINEERING

**Today's technical item:** R-410A production was halted entirely on January 1, 2026 under the EPA's Technology Transitions Rule, which requires all new residential and light commercial HVAC systems to use refrigerants with a Global Warming Potential (GWP) of 700 or less. R-454B (GWP 466) and R-32 (GWP 675) are the primary A2L replacements — both are mildly flammable, requiring updated installer certification, revised equipment enclosures, and leak-detection considerations per ASHRAE 15. For existing R-410A systems, only recycled or reclaimed refrigerant can now be sourced for service top-ups; a 5-lb recharge is approaching $400 in many markets — creating a strong business case for accelerating legacy equipment replacement this season. *(Source: Kele.com, ACHR News, OxMaint — 2026 HVAC refrigerant transition coverage)*
