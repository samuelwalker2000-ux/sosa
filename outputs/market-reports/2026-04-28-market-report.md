### 9. FITNESS

**Today's insight:** A 2026 randomized controlled trial (published in Sports Medicine) confirmed that heavy strength training improves running economy specifically in a fatigued state at marathon race intensity — not just when fresh. The mechanism is neuromuscular efficiency: stronger legs generate the same pace with lower metabolic cost once glycogen-depleted. Practical application: two heavy compound leg sessions per week (squats, Romanian deadlifts, single-leg press) will improve your last-10km race pace more meaningfully than adding easy aerobic mileage at this stage of training.
